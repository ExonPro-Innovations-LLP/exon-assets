## Full Description

**Stop Typing. Start Scanning.**

Your sales team collects dozens of business cards at every event — then spends hours typing contacts into Salesforce. xVio Scan eliminates manual data entry using a two-stage AI pipeline (OCR + LLM).

**How It Works**
1. Scan with your phone camera or upload from gallery
2. AI extracts and structures data into named fields
3. One-tap export to Salesforce as Leads, Contacts, or custom objects

**Why xVio Scan?**

95%+ Accuracy: Two-stage AI extraction including handwritten text recognition.

No Per-User Fees: Org-based pricing — your entire team scans without escalating costs.

Works Offline: Scan at trade shows and field sites, auto-syncs when back online.

Multi-Document: Business cards, invoices, receipts, notes, forms, and custom types.

Campaign Attribution: Tag scans to Salesforce Campaigns for ROI tracking.

Enterprise Ready: SSO, custom branding, zero data retention, multi-tenant isolation.

Available on iOS, Android, and Web (PWA).

## Highlights (up to 9)

1. **Two-Stage AI Extraction** — OCR recognizes text, then LLM structures it into named fields with 95%+ accuracy. Not basic OCR or template matching — real AI intelligence that handles messy handwriting and unusual layouts.

2. **No Per-User Fees** — Pricing is per organization, not per seat. Your entire sales, marketing, and field team can scan without escalating license costs. Free tier included (100 scans/month).

3. **Works Offline** — Full scanning capability at trade shows, conferences, and field sites with poor connectivity. Scans queue locally and auto-sync to Salesforce when back online. Never lose a lead.

4. **Multi-Document Types** — Business cards, invoices, receipts, handwritten notes, forms, ID documents, certificates, and custom document types. One app for all your scanning needs — not just business cards.

5. **One-Tap Salesforce Sync** — Extracted data maps directly to Salesforce as Leads, Contacts, Accounts, or custom objects. No copy-paste, no import files, no middleware. Scan to CRM in seconds.

6. **Zero Data Retention** — Source files are deleted after processing, configurable per organization. Your sensitive documents never linger on external servers. Full encryption at rest and in transit.

7. **Campaign Attribution** — Tag scans to Salesforce Campaigns for end-to-end ROI tracking. Know exactly which events, trade shows, and field activities generate pipeline — from scan to closed deal.

8. **Duplicate Detection** — Smart image hashing prevents accidental re-scans of the same document. No duplicate Leads or Contacts cluttering your CRM.

9. **Enterprise Ready** — SSO (SAML/OIDC), custom branding (white-label with your logo and colors), zero data retention (source files deleted after processing), and multi-tenant data isolation with encryption at rest and in transit.
